Instant Pot Burritos

Description

Instant pot burritos made with black beans and corn!

Ingredients

  • Black beans, drained and rinsed, 1 can
  • Corn, drained, 1 can
  • White rice, 2 cups
  • Taco seasoning, 1/4 cup
  • Chicken breasts, 3 to 4 large breasts, cut into 1-2 inch strips
  • Medium Salsa, 12 oz
  • Water or chicken stock, 2 cups
  • Shredded cheese, To taste
  • Sour cream, To taste

Directions

  1. Rinse rice in strainer until water runs clear.
  2. Pour 1/2 cup of broth/water into the Instant Pot.
  3. In the following order add: chicken breasts, taco seasoning, rice, black beans, corn, and salsa.
  4. Add the remaining 1 and 1/2 cups of broth/water. Use rice paddle to press all ingredients into liquid.
  5. Making sure the rubber ring is secure, secure the lid and set the valve to sealing.
  6. Cook at high pressure on Manual for 10 minutes. After pressure cook is complete, allow NPR for 5 minutes. Release the remaining pressure and remove the lid.
  7. Use tongs to remove chicken breasts. Shred or cut as desired.
  8. Serve in a bowl, on a tortilla, or with chips. Garnish as desired with shredded cheese, sour cream, taco sauce, and cilantro.
